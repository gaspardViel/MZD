# Curriculum

## Active axes

### `skill-craft` — mode: knowledge
Serves the mission by: making the operator fluent in writing the skills (leading words, completion criteria, failure modes, context budget) that reliably produce MZD content.
Lessons: 0002, 0003, 0010, 0011, 0012, 0013

### `coordination` — mode: skills
Serves the mission by: orchestrating the multi-source fetch, parallel reads, scheduled runs and error handling that feed the briefs and summaries.
Lessons: 0004, 0006, 0007, 0008, 0009

### `gouvernance` — mode: wisdom
Serves the mission by: rendering the MZD's real structure (pôles, CA, bénévoles) legible and alive to volunteers.
Lessons: 0005, 0014, 0015, 0016
